Nailed It

HTML5 | CSS3 | JAVASCRIPT

Nailed It is a random nail color selector for those of us who sit in the chair every three weeks and still can't decide what to pick. Randomized with the use of JavaScript, users will be presented with a nail color(s), design, and inspirational quote. If they find themselves still in need, they need only scroll down and look to the gallery wall to help get nailed!
